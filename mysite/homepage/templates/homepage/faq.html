What is MuggleGateway?

MuggleGateway is the digital publishing, entertainment and news company and the global digital publisher of The Harry Potter Wizarding World. As the digital heart of the Wizarding World, mugglegateway.com is dedicated to unlocking the power of imagination. It offers news, features, and articles as well as quizzes and forums.


Is all the content all by J.K. Rowling?

The content includes entertaining articles and the latest news about the Wizarding World from the MuggleGateway team. The latest news articles from newspapers and magazines continues to be at the heart of the new site.


What devices does the MuggleGateway site work on?

MuggleGateway can be enjoyed on multiple devices and screen sizes including mobile phones, tablets, laptops and desktops.


When will I learn what my Patronus would be?

For now, we can't give you an exact date, but we can tell you that the MuggleGateway team is hard at work making this happen.


Does MuggleGateway use affiliate relationships?

As of today, MuggleGateway does not use affiliate relationships. If that changes in the future, all users will be notified.
